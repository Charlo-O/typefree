# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Typefree serves people who write across desktop apps and want to replace repeated typing with speech: developers, operators, writers, and knowledge workers who move between editors, chat, docs, and email. The landing page visitor is deciding whether this tool is fast, trustworthy, and worth installing.

## Product Purpose

Typefree is an open-source desktop dictation app that turns spoken Chinese and English into text that can be pasted into everyday applications. The landing page should communicate speed, platform availability, open-source access, and the practical workflow of speaking instead of typing.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, direct, capable. The voice should feel like a sharp desktop utility: calm enough to trust, fast enough to want, and plain-spoken enough to avoid inflated AI claims.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradient card stacks, exaggerated AI magic language, over-blue or purple tech decoration, and any claim that implies local privacy or private offline processing unless the product explicitly guarantees it. Avoid cluttered comparison tables and decorative effects that make the product feel less practical.

## Design Principles

Show the workflow, not abstractions.
Keep claims concrete and verifiable.
Make the product feel installable within seconds.
Use bilingual copy without making the page feel duplicated.
Let open source and platform downloads carry trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, readable Chinese and English at mobile sizes, keyboard-visible focus states, and reduced-motion-friendly interactions.
